My awesome cupcakes and some recipes.

Chocolate cupcake

Ingredients: 1 1/2 cups flour 1 cup sugar 1 teaspoon baking soda 1 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup cocoa powder 1/2 cup vegetable oil 1 cup water 1 teaspoon vinegar Preheat the oven to 350 F. Pour the dry ingredients into a large bowl and mix. Add the oil, water and vinegar. Use an electric mixer to mix well - the mixture should be smooth with no lumps. Pour mixture into a greased cupcake tin and bake for 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the cupcake comes out clean.

Chocolate frosting

Ingredients: 1 stick (1/2 cup) butter or margarine 2/3 cup Hershey's Cocoa 3 cups powdered sugar 1/3 cup milk 1 teaspoon vanilla extract Melt butter in a bowl. Stir in cocoa. Alternately add powdered sugar and milk, beating to spreading consistency. Add small amount additional milk, if needed. Stir in vanilla.
