About Us

Our History

Our restaurant was born in the flames of passion. Our passion for tasty meat dishes and sweet deserts. So one day we decided to share with the rest of the world the wonderful recipes we have discovered with a bit of style. The rest is a dream come true. We have opened our restaurant in 2014 in the city of Sofia, Bulgaria. Since the opening of the restaurant we came to the conclusion that we need to search for even more recipes. And we did. Today we have a dedicated Recipe Hunter that scouts the world for all these tasty treasures.
