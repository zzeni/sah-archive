Our Services

Buy

You can find great books in our bookstore. We have special offers for you, but that is not everything - you can come to us, sit for some time, have a drink in our place and read a book. There is no need to buy it, if you don't want to. You are always welcome alone or with friends to come and read. Also we organize special readings with famous people. Reading is a gift and we want to share it with you!

Make a present

If you want to help people read books, but you know that not everybody can afford this luxury, there is a way: give us some of your old books. We will give them to people who want to read. In return, you will not only have made something good, but you will receive a voucher for a big discount! It's worth to be a good person, isn't it?!

Give away for a while

You may not want to quit from your books, but still want to help. Then you can leave a book in our shop for a month, so somebody can take, read and give it back. You may worry about the condition in which it will be after that. But we guarantee about it, because if your book has been damaged, you will receive the same new. Also you will receive a voucher for discount!
